Direct/Ducted Evaporative Coolers

World’s Best Performing Evaporative Air Coolers

Arctic Direct Evaporative Coolers are evaporative, industrial, ducted, commercial, air, swamp coolers, industrial ventilation coolers, and centralized air coolers. The Arctic cooler offers an entirely natural way of delivering refreshing cool air while providing excellent, fresh & filtered air with ventilation into the space.

Arctic Direct/Ducted Evaporative Coolers can lower the fresh air dry bulb temperature by 7-12°C, e.g.- the air-off temperature from the evaporative cooler for an outside temperature of 40-43°C can be as low as 28-30°C.

Reduction of temperature in the workplace creates a healthy environment and apparent benefits of improved staff morale, increased productivity & accuracy and less absenteeism.
